I’m Sufi - I imagine and build products that help us say ‘hello, world’, a little better, everyday.

a multidisciplinary approach to product

I look up and around a lot. At billowing, passing clouds. To catch a glimpse of the space station’s small white dot. At user’s faces and reactions, their feelings and moods, finding out what, in turn, they look at. Understanding people and how they interact with the world fascinates me. So do fireworks, which I usually have in my room, and occasionally in my purse.


In the last year I’ve become a jr web dev, walked across northern Spain, and immersed myself in the fog and creativity of SF. Having loved and worked in design, branding, and growth for six years, I wanted to shape and build products that were almost invisible, infusing magic and delight into a user's experience. I would love to add my optimism, energy, and fresh thinking to your project. Let’s get coffee soon. Email me at sufi.k.sidhu@gmail.com.
